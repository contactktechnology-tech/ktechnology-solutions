# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

KTechnology Solutions is committed to maintaining a respectful, professional, and inclusive environment for all contributors, users, and stakeholders.

We value collaboration, technical excellence, and integrity. Participation in this project is subject to the standards outlined below.

---

## Expected Behaviour

All participants are expected to:
- Communicate professionally and respectfully
- Be considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Act in good faith and with technical honesty

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours are not tolerated:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Abusive, threatening, or demeaning language or conduct
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith participation
- Publishing private or confidential information without consent
- Any conduct that undermines trust, safety, or professionalism

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Public and private communications related to the project
- Any situation where an individual represents or engages with the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent bans
- Rejection of contributions
- Removal of access

Decisions are made at the discretion of the maintainers and are final.

---

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, report it confidentially to:
